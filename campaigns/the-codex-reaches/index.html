You enter a vaulted sanctum hung with unlit lanterns. Iolen Vespertine, newly arrived from the Southern Vales, unrolls a vellum pact and lays it on the oak table. Every span of thread across the chamber may be watched, the pact reads, but no name may be spoken. The obsidian die turns up eighteen — the lanterns kindle one after another down both walls.

COMMENT Zero blocking findings; two polish items left on the oak table; four praises carved in the margin. The pact holds.
