The Codex Reaches now spans four regions, seven reviews, eight sessions. In the Southern Vales, the Covenant of Lanterns was sealed on the first afternoon — a pact that permits the Archive to watch every span of thread through those chambers while forbidding the speaking of any subject’s name. The same evening, Iolen Vespertine returned with fresh red wax and struck through both margin-notes. The sanctum holds. One thread lingers in waiting: the watching rites still lack their unit of proof.

The second region is the Conductor’s Exchange — a sorting-house where records are indexed by tongue and audience and dispatched to their rightful product surfaces. Three roads arrived at the gatehouse at the same hour. Mía Guajarde reached the Exchange uninvited, ledger in hand and an iron key at her collar that opens nothing yet identified. She found the fractures in the gate mechanism before they widened, named two correctness blockers without ceremony, and held the line in serious mode. The Exchange holds; the gate has not yet cleared.

The third region sits above running water. At the Harbor of Sigils — a vestibule-and-register above the lower Cistern of Monarch — Kadren Velloway cut a tenth channel into the old stone. The masonry was clean, the seals were drying, and two of them proved unfinished on inspection. A ballot opened at the Harbor resolved toward study; the community chose to examine the underlying contract before touching the masonry again. Kadren descended a second time the same day into the Scrivener’s Undercroft, where the blueprint rang true but two guardian posts had drifted, and the new wing stood without armor. The party chose to press deeper; a reckoning is owed.

At the Courier’s Foundry, Theren Vask crossed the Thread-Marked Corridor to retire a family-forged lantern in favor of the Guild’s standard flame. Three blank stones were sent back to the cutter. The ballot that stood at that threshold fell silent; the dice chose the doctrine of propagation. Iolen Vespertine then visited the Foundry’s outer road to amend a small waystone pact, setting the shrine’s lamp to burn by default. Two small coverage gaps were noted and the traveler was let through.

Most recently, Iolen Vespertine returned to her home region of the Southern Vales under serious mode — the entire covenant between the Archive and its outposts renegotiated from its first word, the Code Mage the sole hand on the stylus. The boundary stones answered in sequence under a roll of 19, but the session did not close clean: one seal was pressed with a hand too loose. The artifact-chamber’s outer door still opens when it should not, and the gatekeeper’s courier, when silent, sends the traveler on without any roles at all. Two clauses were marked in the margin to be redrafted before the next pilgrimage. Seven reviews; ninety-three remain.
