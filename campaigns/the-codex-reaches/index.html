═══╡ > look ╞═══════════════════════════════════════════

The Codex Reaches now spans four regions. In the Southern Vales, the Covenant of Lanterns was sealed on the first afternoon — a pact that permits the Archive to watch every span of thread through those chambers while forbidding the speaking of any subject’s name. The same evening, Iolen Vespertine returned with fresh red wax and struck through both margin-notes. The sanctum holds. One open thread lingers: the new rites await their unit of proof.

The second region is the Conductor’s Exchange — a sorting-house where driver records are indexed by tongue and audience and dispatched to their rightful product surfaces. Three roads arrived at the gatehouse at the same hour. Mía Guajarde reached the Exchange uninvited, ledger in hand and an iron key at her collar that opens nothing yet identified. She found the fractures in the gate mechanism before they widened, named two correctness blockers without ceremony, and held the line in serious mode. The Exchange holds. The gate has not yet cleared.

The third region sits above running water. At the Harbor of Sigils — a vestibule-and-register above the lower Cistern of Monarch — Kadren Velloway cut a tenth channel into the old stone. The masonry was clean, the seals were drying, and two of them proved unfinished on inspection. A ballot opened at the Harbor resolved toward study; the community chose to examine the underlying contract before touching the masonry again.

The fourth region lies deeper still. Kadren descended the same day into the Scrivener’s Undercroft — the record-vaults and schema chambers beneath the Harbor where the blueprints for new integration types are drawn and stored. All checks passed clean and the foundation rang true. Yet two guardians of order had drifted out of alignment with the new type, and an entire new wing of the construction stood without the test armor that its siblings all carry. Careful notes were left. A fresh ballot now awaits the community’s voice.

Four reviews across five sessions. Three characters on the field. Four regions mapped. The obsidian die has not yet turned a legendary twenty; the lowest rolled was an eight at the Undercroft — uneasy passage, but the foundation held. Ninety-six encounters remain.
