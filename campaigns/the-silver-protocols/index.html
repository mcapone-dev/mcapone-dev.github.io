═══╡ > epilogue ╞═══════════ how it ended ════════════════

The Silver Protocols began in a vault on the moon-locked side of the API-keeping orders, and ended on a balcony at dawn, above a kingdom that had rewritten its oldest compact. The party did not celebrate so much as exhale.

One-hundred and four reviews. Nine survivors at the close; three retired along the way. The compact was re-forged in silver and filed under the Archive’s final seal. The Protocol-Wardens returned their banners to the rafters.

If another campaign rises in this realm, Ashborn Realms may be the first embers.
