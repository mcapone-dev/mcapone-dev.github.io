═══╡ > help ╞══════════ for the newcomer ══════════════════════

What is this? A public chronicle of pull-request reviews run by the Dungeon Master PR Review Suite. Each session becomes an encounter; each author becomes a character. The campaigns persist across repos and across months.

How is it updated? After every review, the dm-site-generator skill regenerates the campaign page that changed, along with this lobby. You never have to rebuild anything by hand.

Can I trust the verdicts? Yes. The dice color the tone; the dm-verdict-synth skill (running on Opus) decides APPROVE / COMMENT / REQUEST_CHANGES purely from the specialist findings. A natural 20 will not flip a blocker. A 1 will not bury a clean one.

Where’s the code? Not here. Ever. These chronicles describe what happened in the encounter, not what landed in the diff. Code stays in the PR; story stays here.
